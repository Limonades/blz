Tap into the decentralized database protocol that follows your users

Highest performance

Built on top of swarming technologies, Bluzelle retrieves bits of data in parallel from the closest nodes to reduce latency and achieve the optimal speed.

Unprecedented safety

All the information in the database is replicated based on the concept of swarm computing. The nodes comprising the swarm are geographically dispersed, thus safe from any natural or human-caused disasters.

Near-infinite scalability

With the amount of connected devices exponentially increasing, Bluzelle automatically gains in potential capacity, as any connected device could apply to become a node in the Bluzelle network.
