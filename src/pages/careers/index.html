Principles

Here are the principles that guide our work

Data without centralization

When technological power structures centralize, it’s the world at large that loses. We believe that to benefit everyone, information should be distributed.

Community as a backbone

Join our Slack community of like-minded developers to discuss dApps and the future of decentralized technology as well as be the first to hear about the database release.

Innovating means experimenting

We generally know the direction, but not the end result. It’s through playing with and combining new technologies that we get ahead of the curve.

Problem-solving the real world

We don’t spend time building on top of or fixing the outdated layers of technology. To solve real problems, we always come back to square one: the needs of people.

If you see a good fit between us, send a note to jobs@bluzelle.com with the steps you are taking toward the change you want to see in the world, and maybe we’ll see that fit as well.
