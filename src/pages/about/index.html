Bluzelle shifts the incumbent power structures and brings access to information through decentralization.

Bluzelle strives to level the technological power structures and access to information through decentralization.

Bluzelle was founded in 2014, when Pavel Bains and Neeraj Murarka realized that opportunities to implement blockchain technology in the financial sector were all around them. The time was ripe.

Fast forward to 2018, Bluzelle has successfully completed its massive $19.5M ICO to build the decentralized database for dApp developers with unprecedented security and scale.
